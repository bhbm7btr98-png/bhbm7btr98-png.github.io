Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Elephant POS LLC ("Elephant POS," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Elephant POS website, iPhone app, and iPad app.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Elephant POS, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information. This can include your email address, sign-in method, and authentication details associated with your account, including Sign in with Apple information and Apple private relay email addresses when applicable.
  • Business and setup information. This can include location names, employee names, owner and employee access settings, menu or catalog data, receipt settings, tax settings, and printer configuration data.
  • Transaction and operational data. This can include orders, receipts, refunds, shift records, daily sales reports, receipt numbers, and related business workflow records you create in the app.
  • Payment-related information. For in-person payments, Elephant POS uses third-party payment providers, including Stripe Terminal. We may store limited payment-related records such as payment intent identifiers, refund identifiers, payment status, and related transaction metadata needed to support checkout and refunds. We do not store full card numbers in the app.
  • Device and app data. This can include a device identifier generated by the app, device name, platform, last sign-in time, selected location, remembered sign-in preferences, and local app settings.
  • Printer and hardware data. This can include printer names, IP addresses, targets, connection details, and printer status information for supported printer workflows.
  • Location-related information. If location permission is granted, location-related information may be used to enable supported in-person payment features that require it.
  • Support communications. If you contact us, we may keep your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create and manage accounts;
  • authenticate users and support sign-in, password reset, and account recovery flows;
  • operate point-of-sale workflows, including catalog management, checkout, receipts, employee access, location management, printer setup, reporting, and account deletion;
  • process and support in-person payments and refunds through third-party payment providers;
  • sync business data across supported devices and cloud services;
  • maintain security, prevent misuse, and troubleshoot operational issues; and
  • respond to support requests and communicate about the service.

3. How Information Is Stored

Elephant POS stores information both locally on your device and in cloud services we use to operate the app.

  • On-device storage. The app may store operational data and settings locally on your device, including local order data, printer settings, employee access state, and certain app preferences.
  • Secure storage. Certain credentials and sign-in related values may be stored using iOS Keychain.
  • Cloud storage. Account and operational records may be stored in cloud services such as Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore.

4. Third-Party Services

Elephant POS uses third-party services to operate key features. These providers may process information in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

  • Apple. Used for platform services including Sign in with Apple, App Store subscriptions, and device-level functionality.
  • Google Firebase. Used for authentication and cloud data storage.
  • Stripe. Used for card-present payment, refund, onboarding, and payment account related services.

5. Payments

Payment processing services are provided by third-party payment partners. Elephant POS does not store full payment card numbers in the app. Payment providers may collect and process transaction data needed to complete payments, refunds, compliance checks, and fraud prevention.

6. Bluetooth and Location Permissions

Elephant POS may request Bluetooth and location permissions to support compatible card reader and payment workflows. If you do not grant these permissions, certain hardware or payment features may not function.

7. Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription through Apple, subscription transactions are handled by Apple. Elephant POS may store subscription status and entitlement state needed to determine access to paid features.

8. Data Sharing

We may share information with service providers only as reasonably necessary to operate the app and support the services described above. We do not sell personal information.

9. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You can request deletion through the app's account deletion flow when available.

10. Your Choices

  • You can remove the app from your device.
  • You can use the in-app delete account flow, when available, to request deletion of account data stored by Elephant POS.
