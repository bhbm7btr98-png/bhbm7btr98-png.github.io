Frequently asked questions.

Does Elephant POS run on both iPhone and iPad?

Yes. Elephant POS is built for both iPhone and iPad so staff can work from the device that best fits the counter.

Can I organize products, services, and menu items?

Yes. You can build categories, organize your catalog, and keep items fast for staff to browse and select.

Can Elephant POS support multiple employees and locations?

Yes. Elephant POS includes employee access controls and multi-location support for growing operations.

Does Elephant POS include sales reporting?

Yes. Sales summaries and daily reporting are available directly inside the app and ready to review on either device.

Does Elephant POS support receipt printers?

Yes. Elephant POS is built with receipt printing workflows in mind for real front-counter use.

How do I connect the printers?

Printers are connected by Ethernet or Wi-Fi on the same network as your device. Once the printers are on the network, you can connect to them from Elephant POS settings.

What type of printers can I use?

Elephant POS is currently compatible with Epson receipt printers. More printer support is coming soon.

What happens if the internet connection drops?

Core selling workflows are designed to keep moving locally, while cloud sync and other network-dependent actions can resume when service returns.
